About Chef Hiro

"True hospitality means anticipating a need before it's spoken."

Born in Osaka, Chef Hiro learned that food is a universal language. But when he moved to Toronto, he noticed that too many "authentic" spots felt crowded or inaccessible to some of his friends.

He created Kizuna Dining with a simple mission: Omotenashi (hospitality) without barriers. Whether it's our wheelchair-friendly layout, neurodiverse-affirming quiet zones, or this very screen-reader-optimized menu, every detail is a love letter to our community. You are welcome here.

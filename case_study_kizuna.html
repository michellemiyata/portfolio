Case Study

Restaurant Kizuna App

A seamless dining companion app enhancing the customer experience.

View Live Project

Role

UX/UI Designer

Timeline

4 Weeks

Tools

Figma, HTML/CSS

Context

Concept App

The Overview

The Goal

To design an intuitive restaurant application that simplifies the ordering process, manages reservations, and showcases the menu with high-quality visuals, all while reflecting the restaurant's authentic brand identity.

Key Features

  • Digital Menu Navigation
  • Seamless Reservation System
  • Online Ordering & Checkout
"Connecting tradition with technology for a modern dining experience."

Concept & Development

Phase 01

Menu Design

The menu features dedicated English and Japanese versions to accommodate a diverse range of guests. This bilingual approach ensures clarity and inclusivity, while the interface remains intuitive and visually engaging with clean typography and appetite-stimulating imagery.

Phase 02

Menu Browsing

Focusing on appetising visuals and clear categorization to help users easily browse and select their favorite dishes. The interface is designed to be clean and distraction-free.

Phase 03

Checkout Flow

Streamlining the checkout process to minimize friction. Clear summary views and easy payment options ensure a smooth transition from selection to purchase.

Phase 04

Visual Identity

Incorporating authentic Japanese aesthetic elements with a modern UI approach. The color palette and imagery are carefully chosen to evoke a sense of warmth and reliability.

Reflection

Designing the Kizuna app highlighted the importance of balancing aesthetic appeal with functional simplicity in food service applications. It was a valuable exercise in translating brand values into a digital product.

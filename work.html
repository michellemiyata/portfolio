Puzzle Piece

Emotional Interaction & Accessibility

Role

UX/UI Designer

Timeline

3 Weeks

Tools

Illustrator, Photoshop

Context

Academic Project

About Production

Brief Description

“Puzzle Piece” is an emotional-design visual exploring connection, alignment, and the quiet moment when something finally “clicks.” The piece is divided into four stages that reflect an inner emotional progression, ending with Piece 315: THE CLICK, representing grounded clarity.

Creative Problem

The challenge was to visually communicate internal emotional alignment without animation or interaction. The project needed to express subtle emotional resonance using only visual cues like composition, puzzle pieces, palette, and layout.

Solution to the Problem

Puzzle pieces became the metaphor for emotional clarity. Each part of the series represents a stage of emotional understanding — from confusion to alignment. Soft tones, minimal layouts, and quiet spacing were used to reinforce the gentle, introspective nature of the project.

Process & Development

Miro Board

Early ideas, emotional notes, arrangement tests, and structural planning were mapped out in Miro. This foundation helped shape the emotional arc.

Rough Versions

Early visual drafts explored spacing, contrast, puzzle-piece sizing, and softness of tone.

Screen Captures

Screenshots during the Illustrator/Photoshop process showing colour tuning, alignment, layer ordering, and detail refinement.

Reference Photos

A few stock photos were used for lighting, palette, and texture reference. No AI images were used in this project.

The Challenge

The main challenge was expressing a quiet emotional moment — the feeling of alignment — using only still visuals rather than animation or interaction.

The Concept

“Puzzle Piece” visualizes emotional clarity using two puzzle pieces that represent alignment and grounded realization. The title “Piece 315 — THE CLICK” symbolizes a moment of internal resonance.

Emotional Journey: Finch to Union — Line 1 TTC

Visual Storytelling & Emotional Design

Role

UX/UI Designer

Timeline

2 Weeks

Tools

Illustrator, Photoshop

Context

Academic Project

About the Production

Brief Description

This project reimagines the TTC Line 1 commute as an emotional timeline rather than a physical map. Each station from Finch to Union is represented by emotional colour-coded moods that reflect the shifting internal experience of commuting.

Creative Problem

Traditional transit maps communicate routes, but not the emotional reality of navigating crowded TTC environments. The challenge was to interpret a familiar public system through a more human and emotional lens.

Solution to the Problem

Using colour-coded emotions inspired by the clarity of the NYC Vignelli map, each TTC station is translated into a mood. The final visual becomes an emotional map of the rider’s inner journey.

Process & Development

Development focused on emotional research, colour studies, and a Miro board that mapped how TTC stations relate to emotional shifts throughout the commute.

Emotional Colour Framework

Inspired by the NYC Vignelli subway map, emotions were assigned colours to represent transitions throughout the journey:

  • Yellow — Happiness / relief
  • Blue — Sadness / heaviness
  • Red — Anger / overwhelm
  • Black — Fear / anxiety
  • Purple — Surprise / awakening

Emotional colour references inspired by NYC Vignelli design.

Process Mapping in Miro

The Miro board mapped emotional pacing, transitions, and the meaningful emotional differences between each TTC station.

Miro board used to map emotional transitions.

Early Digital Layout Exploration

Early digital drafts helped refine pacing, spacing, colour shifts, and TTC-inspired structure.

Early digital variations showing emotional sequencing.

Station-by-Station Emotional Notes

Notes were documented for each TTC station to better understand how the commute feels — not just how it looks physically.

  • Finch → Sheppard — anticipation, quiet nerves
  • Sheppard → Eglinton — heaviness, routine
  • Eglinton → Bloor — emotional noise, overwhelm
  • Bloor → Union — grounding, soft return to self

The Result

The final emotional map transforms the Finch → Union TTC journey into a soft, colour-coded emotional timeline that visualizes the inner commute.

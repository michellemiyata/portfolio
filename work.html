Emotional Interaction & Accessibility

Role

UX/UI Designer

Timeline

3 Weeks

Tools

Illustrator, Photoshop

Context

Academic Project

About Production

Brief Description

“Puzzle Piece” is an emotional-design visual exploring connection, alignment, and the quiet moment when something finally “clicks.” The piece is divided into four stages that reflect an inner emotional progression, ending with Piece 315: THE CLICK, representing grounded clarity.

Creative Problem

The challenge was to visually communicate internal emotional alignment without animation or interaction. The project needed to express subtle emotional resonance using only visual cues like composition, puzzle pieces, palette, and layout.

Solution to the Problem

Puzzle pieces became the metaphor for emotional clarity. Each part of the series represents a stage of emotional understanding — from confusion to alignment. Soft tones, minimal layouts, and quiet spacing were used to reinforce the gentle, introspective nature of the project.

Process & Development

Miro Board

Early ideas, emotional notes, arrangement tests, and structural planning were mapped out in Miro. This foundation helped shape the emotional arc.

Rough Versions

Early visual drafts explored spacing, contrast, puzzle-piece sizing, and softness of tone.

Screen Captures

Screenshots during the Illustrator/Photoshop process showing colour tuning, alignment, layer ordering, and detail refinement.

Reference Photos

A few stock photos were used for lighting, palette, and texture reference. No AI images were used in this project.

The Challenge

The main challenge was expressing a quiet emotional moment — the feeling of alignment — using only still visuals rather than animation or interaction.

The Concept

“Puzzle Piece” visualizes emotional clarity using two puzzle pieces that represent alignment and grounded realization. The title “Piece 315 — THE CLICK” symbolizes a moment of internal resonance.

Hi, I'm Michelle.

I’m a UX/UI designer who believes digital spaces should feel as welcoming as physical ones.

I specialize in emotional design and inclusive research, crafting experiences that gently guide users through complex moments. I love paying attention to the small shifts in user behavior—the things people feel but don't always say.

Ultimately, I design to bring a sense of calm and care to everyday technology.

Hi, I'm Michelle.

I’m a focused Digital Experience Designer who builds interactions that feel gentle, thoughtful, and human.

My work blends rigorous research, emotional design, and inclusive principles to support users in the moments they need clarity the most. I design by paying attention to feeling — the small shifts, the emotional cues, the quiet needs people don’t always say out loud.

My goal is to create technology that meets people where they are, with calm and care.
